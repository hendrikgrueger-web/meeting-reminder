### Description (max 4000 chars)

```
QuickJoin lives in your menu bar and makes sure you never miss a video call. When a meeting is about to start, a beautiful full-screen overlay appears — showing the meeting title, countdown, and a one-click Join button that opens your video conferencing app instantly.

No more hunting for links in your calendar. No more "sorry, I'm late" moments.

─────────────────────────────────
NEVER MISS A MEETING
─────────────────────────────────

• Full-screen overlay with countdown timer and LIVE badge
• Configurable reminder lead time: 1, 2, 3, or 5 minutes before start
• Snooze for 1 minute when you're not quite ready
• Keyboard shortcuts: Return to join, Escape to dismiss, Space to snooze
• Screen-sharing aware: shows a system notification instead of covering your screen when you're presenting

─────────────────────────────────
ONE-CLICK JOIN FOR 8 PROVIDERS
─────────────────────────────────

QuickJoin automatically detects meeting links from:

• Microsoft Teams
• Zoom
• Google Meet
• Cisco WebEx
• GoTo Meeting
• Slack Huddles
• Whereby
• Jitsi Meet

Links are extracted from your calendar event's location, notes, and URL — no manual setup needed.

─────────────────────────────────
SMART AGENDA VIEW
─────────────────────────────────

• Today's meeting overview right in the menu bar
• Calendars grouped by account for easy toggling
• Filter to show only meetings with a video link
• Global shortcut (⌘⇧J) to open the agenda from anywhere
• Dynamic menu bar icon: shows a badge when a meeting is coming up soon

─────────────────────────────────
PRIVACY FIRST
─────────────────────────────────

QuickJoin runs entirely on your device. Your calendar data never leaves your Mac — no servers, no tracking, no analytics. Calendar access is read-only and stays local.

─────────────────────────────────
LIGHTWEIGHT BY DESIGN
─────────────────────────────────

• 0% CPU in idle — event-driven scheduling, no polling
• Less than 20 MB memory footprint
• Instant startup
• Handles Mac sleep/wake: catches meetings you might have missed

─────────────────────────────────
QUICKJOIN PREMIUM
─────────────────────────────────

QuickJoin is free for your first 50 meeting reminders. After that, QuickJoin Premium keeps the reminders coming:

• Monthly: $0.99 / month
• Annual: $7.99 / year (save 33%)

Premium is an auto-renewable subscription. Payment is charged to your Apple ID at confirmation of purchase. Subscription automatically renews unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage and cancel your subscription in your App Store account settings at any time.

─────────────────────────────────
REQUIREMENTS
─────────────────────────────────

• macOS 15 or later
• Calendar access (read-only)
```
