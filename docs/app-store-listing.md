### Description (max 4000 chars)

```
Nevr Late lives in your menu bar and makes sure you never miss a video call again. When a meeting is about to start, a stunning full-screen overlay appears — showing the meeting title, countdown, and a one-click Join button that launches your video app instantly.

No more hunting for links in your calendar. No more "sorry, I'm a minute late."

─────────────────────────────────
NEVER MISS A MEETING
─────────────────────────────────

• Full-screen overlay with live countdown and LIVE badge
• Configurable reminder lead time: 1 to 10 minutes before start
• Snooze for 1 minute when you're not quite ready
• Keyboard shortcuts: Return to join, Escape to dismiss, Space to snooze
• Screen-sharing aware: sends a system notification instead of covering your screen while you present

─────────────────────────────────
ONE-CLICK JOIN FOR 8 PROVIDERS
─────────────────────────────────

Nevr Late automatically detects meeting links from:

• Microsoft Teams (including GCC and Government)
• Zoom (all regional subdomains)
• Google Meet
• Cisco WebEx
• GoTo Meeting
• Slack Huddles
• Whereby
• Jitsi Meet

Links are extracted from your calendar event's location, notes, and URL — no manual setup, no copy-pasting.

─────────────────────────────────
SMART DAILY AGENDA
─────────────────────────────────

• Today's full meeting schedule right in the menu bar
• Calendars grouped by account — toggle any calendar with one click
• Filter to show only meetings with a video link
• Global shortcut (⌘⇧J) opens the next meeting from anywhere on your Mac
• Dynamic menu bar icon with badge counter when meetings are coming up

─────────────────────────────────
PRIVACY FIRST
─────────────────────────────────

Nevr Late runs entirely on your device. Your calendar data never leaves your Mac — no servers, no tracking, no analytics. Calendar access is read-only and stays 100% local.

─────────────────────────────────
LIGHTWEIGHT BY DESIGN
─────────────────────────────────

• 0% CPU in idle — event-driven scheduling, zero polling
• Under 20 MB memory footprint
• Instant startup, no login required
• Handles Mac sleep/wake: catches meetings you might have missed when your MacBook wakes up

─────────────────────────────────
NEVR LATE PREMIUM
─────────────────────────────────

Nevr Late is free for your first 50 meeting reminders — no account, no sign-up. After that, Nevr Late Premium keeps the reminders coming indefinitely:

• Monthly: €0.99 / month
• Annual: €7.99 / year (save 33%)

Nevr Late Premium is an auto-renewable subscription. Payment is charged to your Apple ID at confirmation of purchase. The subscription automatically renews unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage and cancel your subscription at any time in your App Store account settings.

─────────────────────────────────
REQUIREMENTS
─────────────────────────────────

• macOS 26 or later
• Calendar access (read-only)
```
