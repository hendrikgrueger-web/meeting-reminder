Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

Overview

QuickJoin ("the App") is a macOS menu bar application developed by Hendrik Grüger. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is handled.

Data Collection

QuickJoin does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App does not use analytics, tracking, or any third-party services.

Calendar Access

QuickJoin requests read-only access to your macOS calendars via Apple's EventKit framework. This access is used solely to:

  • Display upcoming meeting events
  • Extract meeting join links (Teams, Zoom, Google Meet, WebEx, GoTo, Slack, Whereby, Jitsi)
  • Schedule reminder notifications

Calendar data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Network Access

QuickJoin does not make any network requests. The App has no network entitlements. When you click "Join", the meeting URL is opened via macOS's standard URL handler (NSWorkspace), which hands it to the appropriate app (e.g., Microsoft Teams, Zoom).

Data Storage

The App stores only your preferences (selected calendars, reminder timing, sound settings) using macOS UserDefaults. No calendar data is persisted.

Third-Party Services

QuickJoin does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
